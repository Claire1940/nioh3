Frequently Asked Questions

Hellmart is a survival horror game where you work the night shift at a 24-hour convenience store in a remote northern town. During the day, you manage retail operations—stocking shelves, serving customers, and meeting sales quotas. At night, the store becomes a nightmare as you defend against supernatural threats while trying to identify which customers are actually monsters in disguise.

Hellmart features a 7-day campaign where you work consecutive shifts at the haunted supermarket. Each day increases in difficulty, with more challenging quotas during the day and more dangerous threats at night. Your choices throughout all 7 days determine which ending you unlock.

Hellmart has four main endings: Good Ending, Bad Ending, Secret Ending, and True Ending. Each ending has specific requirements based on your choices throughout the 7 days. The Secret and True Endings have hidden requirements that most players miss on their first playthrough.

To survive the night shift, you must prepare during the day by managing resources, then use the generator, security cameras, and barricade doors effectively at night. Never step outside during night hours, learn to identify monsters disguised as customers, and prioritize power management to keep critical systems running.

The quota system requires you to meet daily sales targets during your day shift. You must balance stocking shelves, serving customers, and maintaining the store to generate enough revenue. Failing to meet quota can impact your resources for night survival and affect which endings you can unlock.

Monsters disguised as customers exhibit subtle behavioral differences, facial anomalies, and unusual speech patterns. Pay attention to customer movements, listen for audio cues, use security cameras to observe behavior, and trust your instincts when something feels wrong. Our complete creature guide provides detailed identification tips.

Hellmart is currently available on PC via Steam. The game was released on January 28, 2026. Console versions for PS5, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch have not been officially announced, but players have expressed interest in console ports.

A single playthrough of Hellmart takes approximately 3-5 hours to complete all 7 days. However, unlocking all endings, finding all secrets, and achieving 100% completion can take 10-15 hours depending on your playstyle and familiarity with the game mechanics.

During the day shift, prioritize tasks in this order: 1) Meet minimum quota requirements, 2) Prepare for night shift (check generator, plan barricades), 3) Stock high-profit items, 4) Maintain store cleanliness. Don't waste time perfecting the store if it means being unprepared for night survival.
