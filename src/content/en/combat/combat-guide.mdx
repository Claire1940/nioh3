## Key Tips to Mastering Nioh 3 Combat

- **Experiment with Your Loadout**: The right weapons and armor will significantly impact your combat performance. For example, using a fast weapon like a dual katana in Low Stance allows you to chain multiple fast attacks, while a heavy weapon in High Stance will help you land devastating blows.
- **Master Dodging and Blocking**: Nioh 3 rewards players who understand how to dodge and block attacks effectively. A well-timed dodge can allow you to quickly reposition and attack an enemy’s weak points.
- **Adapt to Enemy Patterns**: Every enemy in Nioh 3 has its own attack pattern, and learning these patterns will help you identify when to attack and when to defend. By carefully observing their movements, you can exploit openings for deadly combos.

## Advanced Combat Strategies for Nioh 3

For players looking to take their combat skills to the next level, here are a few advanced tips:

1. **Stance Switching in Combat**: Many experienced players recommend switching stances during a combo to throw off enemies. For example, start in High Stance to break an enemy’s guard, then switch to Low Stance to finish with fast, precise strikes.
2. **Use Your Skills Wisely**: Skills such as those from the Ninja and Samurai styles can significantly enhance your combat. Players can use these to apply buffs or create openings in combat. For example, using a stealth skill can allow you to land an opening attack before your enemy even realizes you're there.
3. **Understand the Enemy's Weaknesses**: Certain enemies are weak to specific types of attacks. Experiment with elemental weapons or use skills that target an enemy’s weak spots for maximum damage.
