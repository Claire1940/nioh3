Nioh 3 is a game brimming with intricate systems, and mastering its combat mechanics is crucial to progressing. One of the most important aspects of that combat is the skills system, where players can enhance their abilities to match their playstyle. But with so many options, where should you begin? In this guide, we’ll walk you through the best **Nioh 3 skills** to unlock first, ensuring you gain the upper hand from the very start.

### Why the Right Skills Matter in Nioh 3

When diving into **Nioh 3**, players are faced with a massive skill tree, featuring multiple branches and playstyles. Whether you're a swordsman, a ninja, or a hybrid warrior, your skills will determine your effectiveness in battle. By choosing the best **Nioh 3 skills** early, you ensure you're not left struggling during tougher fights. Moreover, certain skills can help make the gameplay smoother and more enjoyable, especially for newcomers. Unlocking the right abilities early can significantly impact your progress and make the game's difficulty feel more manageable.

### Top Skills to Unlock in Nioh 3

Here’s a rundown of the most valuable **Nioh 3 skills** to prioritize in your skill tree. These choices are based on community feedback, experience from seasoned players, and their ability to offer both offensive and defensive advantages.

#### 1. **Ki Pulse Mastery**
Ki Pulse is one of the most essential mechanics in Nioh 3. When executed correctly, it replenishes your stamina (or Ki), allowing for more attacks and better defense. Ki Pulse Mastery boosts the efficiency of your Ki Pulse, making it more effective and reducing the Ki cost during combat. For beginners, this is a must-have, as it allows for extended battles without running out of stamina.

- **Why Unlock First?** This skill allows you to maintain momentum in battles, crucial for both offense and defense. It’s particularly helpful against stronger enemies who can wear you down quickly.

#### 2. **Fighting Spirit**
This is a passive skill that increases your focus, giving you a significant boost in your fighting potential. Unlocking Fighting Spirit early improves your overall survivability by making it easier to chain attacks together without running out of Ki.

- **Why Unlock First?** Fighting Spirit enhances your ability to stay aggressive in fights without worrying about draining your Ki too quickly. It's one of the most vital **Nioh 3 skills** for maintaining pressure on enemies.

#### 3. **Quickstep**
Quickstep is a mobility-based skill that allows you to dodge attacks more effectively. It provides faster movement, allowing you to reposition quickly to either avoid danger or land critical hits.

- **Why Unlock First?** In a game like **Nioh 3**, where dodging is often the key to survival, Quickstep is indispensable. It helps you avoid damage and lets you stay mobile in chaotic fights. Plus, the faster you can react, the better your chances are to counterattack.

#### 4. **Dual Sword Mastery**
If you favor dual katanas or twin swords, Dual Sword Mastery should be one of your first unlocks. This skill increases the effectiveness of your attacks and combos, making your damage output soar.

- **Why Unlock First?** Dual Sword Mastery significantly boosts your DPS (damage per second), which is vital in dealing with multiple enemies and bosses. This skill is essential for those focusing on agility and fast-paced combat.

#### 5. **Ninjutsu - Shuriken**
For those who prefer stealthy tactics or ranged attacks, unlocking Shuriken under the Ninjutsu skills is highly recommended. Shuriken are throwable projectiles that can deal damage from a distance, allowing you to weaken enemies before closing in.

- **Why Unlock First?** Shuriken can be especially useful in the early stages of **Nioh 3**, helping you break enemy posture or distract them while you move in for a more powerful attack.

#### 6. **Guarding Mastery**
Guarding is an important defensive skill in Nioh 3. With Guarding Mastery, your blocking ability is enhanced, and you can reduce incoming damage more efficiently.

- **Why Unlock First?** Guarding Mastery allows you to stay in the fight longer by minimizing damage, especially against powerful enemies and bosses. It’s a great defensive choice that should not be overlooked.

#### 7. **Odachi Weapon Skills**
For players who opt for the massive Odachi sword, unlocking its weapon skills early on is essential. These skills allow for devastating attacks and combos, perfect for crowd control and high-damage output.

- **Why Unlock First?** The Odachi has some of the most powerful abilities in the game, and being able to unlock them early gives you an edge in battles, particularly against multiple enemies at once.

### Bonus Skills: For Advanced Players

For those who have some experience in the game or are looking to expand their repertoire, here are additional **Nioh 3 skills** worth considering.

- **Burst Counter**: A high-level skill that allows you to perform a devastating counter after an enemy's attack.
- **Elemental Affinity**: Great for those looking to mix elemental effects into their playstyle for added damage.

### How to Spend Your Skill Points Wisely

As with any game with an extensive skill tree, it’s easy to get overwhelmed by all the choices. Here are some tips on how to spend your points wisely in **Nioh 3**:

- Focus on key abilities that suit your preferred playstyle (melee, ranged, or mixed combat).
- Prioritize unlocking skills that enhance your Ki regeneration, mobility, and damage output.
- Don’t forget to level up the defensive skills, as they provide better survivability, especially during boss fights.

### Conclusion

The right **Nioh 3 skills** can make all the difference in your playthrough. By focusing on vital skills like Ki Pulse Mastery, Quickstep, and Dual Sword Mastery, you’ll be able to breeze through the early challenges and prepare yourself for more difficult enemies. Keep experimenting and adapting your skill choices based on your evolving needs in battle.
