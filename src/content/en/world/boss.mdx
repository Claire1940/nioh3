### How to Defeat Each Major Boss in Nioh 3

Here’s an overview of how to approach some of the most challenging **Nioh 3 bosses**:

#### 1. **The First Boss: Tokugawa Kunimatsu**
This tutorial boss is an excellent place to practice your skills. While he’s not too tough, learning how to deflect his attacks and timing your counters will be crucial for later fights.

- **Tip:** Use your basic attacks and watch his wind-up animations. His combos are slow enough to deflect and retaliate.

#### 2. **Yamagata Masakage**
Known for his aggressive stance and deadly charge attacks, Masakage can overwhelm unprepared players. The key is patience—wait for his attacks, deflect, and then counter.

- **Tip:** His charge attack can be dodged or deflected, leaving him open for a combo. Don’t rush the fight; wait for the opening.

#### 3. **Jakotsu-Baba**
This tricky boss can be difficult to approach due to her range and mobility. The best tactic is to wait for her to make a move and then capitalize on the opening.

- **Tip:** Focus on blocking or evading her ranged attacks and close in when she’s vulnerable. A quick burst of attacks will quickly bring her down.

#### 4. **Bloodedge Demon**
A fearsome opponent who uses both elemental attacks and close-range slashes. His moves are fast, so staying agile and controlling the pace of the fight is important.

- **Tip:** Use your fastest weapon for quick attacks and avoid getting caught in his elemental AoE moves. Dodge often and keep your distance.

#### 5. **Baba Nobuharu**
This boss has a lot of reach and powerful strikes. You’ll need to use evasion and precise counter-attacks to take him down without taking damage.

- **Tip:** Wait for his larger wind-ups, then counter with your most powerful attack. Focus on staying behind him to avoid the worst of his wide-reaching moves.
