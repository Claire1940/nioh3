## What Are Yokai in Nioh 3?

In Nioh 3, yokai are supernatural enemies inspired by traditional Japanese mythology. These creatures come in various forms, from grotesque monsters to elemental spirits. They often wield devastating powers that can challenge even the most experienced players. Facing these enemies can be overwhelming, but with the right strategies, you'll be able to overcome them.

## Key Strategies for Fighting Yokai

To tackle the yokai in Nioh 3 effectively, you need to approach each one differently. While some yokai rely on powerful close-range attacks, others may use long-range magic or summon minions to overwhelm you. Here's a general breakdown of strategies that apply to most yokai:

- **Learn Their Moves**: Each yokai has a set of unique attacks. Spend time learning their patterns so you can anticipate their next move.
- **Use the Right Guardian Spirit**: Some yokai are weak against specific Guardian Spirits, so equip the one that offers advantages against their attacks.
- **Focus on Ki**: Many yokai will drain your Ki if you're not careful. Be mindful of your Ki bar and keep an eye on your opponent's stamina to time your attacks effectively.

## Most Notable Yokai in Nioh 3

Several yokai stand out due to their difficulty or unique abilities. Here’s a list of the most notable ones you’ll encounter in Nioh 3:

### 1. **Mezuki**
Mezuki are powerful yokai known for their monstrous size and brutal attacks. Their charge attack can be devastating if you’re not quick on your feet. To defeat them, focus on dodging and attacking their weak points, typically located on their backs or heads.

### 2. **Tengu**
These bird-like yokai are nimble and deadly. Tengu are known for their aerial attacks and quick ground-based strikes. When fighting Tengu, maintain distance to avoid getting caught in their flurry of attacks. Blocking and deflecting their projectiles will also prevent you from taking unnecessary damage.

### 3. **Oni**
The Oni are among the most powerful yokai in Nioh 3. These massive, demon-like enemies have immense health pools and attack power. To deal with them, focus on breaking their guard with heavy attacks and exploit their slow movements to land critical hits.

### 4. **Kappa**
Kappa are tricky and elusive, often hiding in bodies of water or waiting for you to approach. These water creatures can teleport and use water-based magic, making them a challenge to pin down. Equip fire-based weapons to take advantage of their weakness to flames.

### 5. **Yuki-onna**
The Yuki-onna, or "snow woman," is a chilling foe. These ethereal beings can freeze you in place with their ice-based attacks. Make sure to keep moving and use fire-based attacks or the right Guardian Spirits to melt their icy defenses.

## How to Prepare for Yokai Battles

Before heading into battle with a yokai, it’s essential to make sure you're properly equipped. Here are some tips to ensure you're ready:

- **Level Up Your Stats**: Focus on increasing your stamina and resistance to elemental damage. This will help you withstand the yokai's attacks.
- **Use Buffs and Items**: Consumables like healing items, buffs, and traps can be life-savers during yokai encounters. Stock up before venturing into new areas.
- **Enhance Your Weapons**: Yokai are tough, and you’ll need to be at the top of your game. Upgrade your weapons and armor regularly to stay ahead of the curve.

## Yokai Boss Fights

Some of the toughest battles in Nioh 3 involve yokai bosses, which are much more challenging than their regular counterparts. Here are a few notable yokai boss fights:

- **Jakotsu Baba**: This boss uses a combination of poison and ranged attacks, requiring you to stay on your toes. Aim to take out her minions first, then focus on dodging her poison attacks to avoid being overwhelmed.
- **Gaki Chief**: The Gaki Chief commands an army of lesser Gaki enemies. The key to defeating this boss is dealing with the minions quickly and then focusing on the chief itself. Its powerful charging attack can be avoided with timely dodges.
  
By employing the right tactics and preparing thoroughly, you can conquer these mighty foes.
