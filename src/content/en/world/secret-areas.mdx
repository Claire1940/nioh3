## The Most Rewarding Secret Areas in Nioh 3

Here are some of the best-hidden locations in *Nioh 3* that you shouldn’t miss:

### 1. The Forgotten Shrine

The Forgotten Shrine is a hidden temple that can be accessed through a series of platforms located behind a waterfall in the *Ise Province*. According to community reports, this area is home to rare items and offers a boss fight that unlocks special armor once defeated.

- **How to find it**: Look for a waterfall near the western edge of Ise Province, and navigate to a set of platforms behind it.
- **What’s hidden inside**: The Forgotten Shrine rewards players with rare weapons and a challenging boss that grants valuable gear for later stages in the game.

### 2. The Hidden Yokai Cave

This secret cave is located in *Mino Province* and can be accessed by finding a hidden path behind a large rock formation near the outskirts of the area. The cave houses dangerous Yokai, but it also contains an *Amrita Crystal*—a precious item that boosts your level quickly.

- **How to find it**: Search near the edge of Mino’s cliffside for a narrow, seemingly closed-off path that leads into the cave.
- **What’s hidden inside**: Besides the *Amrita Crystal*, there are hidden treasures and one of the hardest Yokai fights that will test your skills.

### 3. The Lost Fortress

In the *Kaga Region*, there’s an area known as the Lost Fortress, often missed due to the hidden nature of the entrance. To find it, you must explore an ancient ruin and look carefully for a crack in the wall leading underground.

- **How to find it**: Follow the ruins toward the back of the area, and inspect the hidden crack in the stone wall. This will lead you down into a dark, forgotten area.
- **What’s hidden inside**: The fortress contains high-level enemies and valuable weapons such as *The Phoenix Blade*—a weapon that can only be obtained by defeating a unique boss in this area.

### 4. The Shadow Temple

The Shadow Temple in *Nioh 3* is hidden in the *Tosa Prefecture* area. Players often miss this secret area, as it requires finding a specific lever hidden in plain sight under an overhanging ledge. Once activated, the lever opens a secret door that leads you into a dungeon full of traps and ancient guardians.

- **How to find it**: Locate the lever under the ledge to the left of the main temple’s front entrance. Pull the lever, and the secret door will reveal itself.
- **What’s hidden inside**: Players can find powerful Amrita-enhancing items here, including a rare Guardian Spirit that boosts elemental damage.
