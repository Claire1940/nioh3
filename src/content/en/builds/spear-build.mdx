### Why is the Spear Build So Powerful in Nioh 3?

The **Nioh 3 spear build** offers players a unique balance of long-range and quick melee combat options. Spear users excel at controlling the battlefield, keeping enemies at bay while also being able to dish out massive damage from a distance. As **Nioh 3** evolves, the spear remains a top-tier weapon choice for players seeking both offense and defense. This build caters to players who want to tackle the game’s most challenging foes from a safe distance without sacrificing power.

### Core Stats for Your Nioh 3 Spear Build

When building your spear-wielding character in **Nioh 3**, certain stats will be crucial to maximize your damage and survivability. Here's a breakdown of the core stats you'll want to focus on:

1. **Strength** – Boosts your overall damage output with the spear, making it essential for increasing your effectiveness in battle.
2. **Skill** – A key stat for enhancing your spear’s combo potential, allowing you to perform faster, more devastating attacks.
3. **Dexterity** – Increases your accuracy and critical hit rate, essential for landing those high-damage attacks at range.
4. **Constitution** – While not as crucial as the other stats, Constitution will help boost your health and give you the necessary durability for tougher encounters.

Invest heavily in **Strength** and **Skill** for the bulk of your damage, while **Dexterity** will ensure you stay accurate from afar. **Constitution** is more of a secondary stat, useful for giving you some extra leeway in fights.

### Key Weapons and Skills for the Spear Build

For the **Nioh 3 spear build**, selecting the right weapon and skills is essential. Here are the best choices:

- **Spear Weapon** – Of course, the Spear itself is the go-to weapon for this build. The spear excels in **long-range combat** and **wide-area attacks** that can hit multiple enemies at once.
- **Severing Spin** – One of the most powerful skills for the spear in **Nioh 3**, Severing Spin offers massive AoE (area-of-effect) damage, making it perfect for clearing groups of enemies from a distance.
- **Quickstep** – This skill will allow you to swiftly dodge incoming attacks while positioning yourself for the next strike, essential for maintaining distance during fights.
- **Spear Dance** – A powerful combo that deals a series of swift attacks, useful when you're surrounded and need to clear a path.

### Best Armor and Gear for the Spear Build

While focusing on **damage output** is important, don't forget to prioritize **defense** and **stamina management** to ensure that you're able to keep fighting without getting overwhelmed. Here are some armor and gear recommendations:

1. **High Dexterity Armor** – Equip armor that enhances your **Dexterity** and **Skill**, helping you stay agile in combat while boosting your damage potential.
2. **Light Armor Set** – Consider using a light armor set to maintain high mobility, which is crucial for dodging incoming attacks when you're keeping enemies at a distance.
3. **Guardian Spirits** – Pick a **Guardian Spirit** that complements your spear build. The **Tengu** Guardian Spirit is a good choice for boosting your range and critical hit damage.

### Nioh 3 Spear Build Combat Strategies

When using the **spear in Nioh 3**, your main focus should be on controlling the battlefield from a distance. Here are some combat strategies to help you maximize your spear build’s potential:

1. **Keep Distance** – Your spear is made for long-range combat. Always keep your enemies at a distance to capitalize on the spear's reach. Use ranged attacks like **Severing Spin** and **Quickstep** to create space and avoid being caught in melee combat.
2. **Control the Crowd** – The spear excels at crowd control. Use your AoE abilities to clear groups of enemies quickly. This is especially useful when facing multiple enemies at once.
3. **Positioning is Key** – Take advantage of your spear's range and positioning. Always try to stay behind your enemies, avoiding their attacks while dealing consistent damage.

If you're ready to take your **Nioh 3 spear build** to the next level, here are some advanced tips:
