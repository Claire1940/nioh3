**Nioh 3 Tonfa Build - Fast & Furious Combat Style**

This build offers high mobility, rapid attacks, and a toolkit of moves that allow for massive damage output while dodging enemies' counters. If you’re seeking a build that rewards fast reflexes and aggressive offense, the Tonfa is the way to go.

Not only does the **Nioh 3 Tonfa build** excel in speed, but it also has a diverse set of abilities that work in both offensive and defensive scenarios. Players can manipulate the flow of combat with devastating combos and techniques, allowing them to stay in control at all times.

### Building Your Nioh 3 Tonfa Build

Creating an effective **Tonfa build** in **Nioh 3** requires some careful planning. You'll need to focus on the right stats, skills, and equipment to maximize your damage and survivability. Here’s a breakdown of key elements for the perfect Tonfa build:

#### Key Stats to Prioritize

- **Skill (Primary Stat)**: Tonfas rely heavily on the **Skill** stat for faster attack speeds and better combos. Prioritize boosting this stat for rapid striking power.
- **Stamina (Secondary Stat)**: Since Tonfas are a fast weapon, having a solid stamina pool allows for continuous attacks without getting exhausted. Invest here to improve your mobility.
- **Heart (Optional)**: Some players prefer to add **Heart** to increase their Ki, which allows them to perform more aggressive attacks without running out of energy too quickly.

#### Essential Skills and Techniques

When selecting skills for your **Nioh 3 Tonfa build**, there are a few that stand out:

- **Tonfa Skill Mastery**: Unlocking these skills allows you to perform quicker, more devastating combos, increasing your damage output significantly.
- **Quickdraw Tonfa**: This skill grants you an initial burst of speed right from the start of combat, which is perfect for closing distance with enemies.
- **Soul Core Abilities**: The **Tonfa build** benefits greatly from certain **Soul Cores** like the **Kirin** or **Tengu**, which enhance mobility and Ki management.

#### Recommended Equipment and Armor

- **Tonfa (Weapon)**: Naturally, the **Tonfa** is your go-to weapon. Look for Tonfas with **high Skill scaling** to maximize your damage potential.
- **Light Armor**: Since the Tonfa build focuses on speed, wearing **Light Armor** allows for maximum mobility. It also lets you dodge attacks faster, keeping you agile.
- **Amulets and Accessories**: Equip **Amulets** that boost your Skill or stamina regeneration. This will help you keep up the fast pace without exhausting your Ki too quickly.

#### Combat Style Tips

- **Keep Moving**: The Tonfa build thrives on **constant movement**. Always keep the pressure on your enemies by utilizing rapid strikes, then immediately following up with evasive maneuvers.
- **Combos and Chain Attacks**: Tonfas excel when you chain multiple attacks together. Utilize **combo finishers** and **quick strikes** to overwhelm your foes before they can react.
- **Defensive Options**: While the **Tonfa build** is aggressive, don’t forget about your defense. Utilize the **Perfect Parry** mechanic and the **Ki Pulse** to manage your stamina and stay in control of the fight.

The **Tonfa build** shines when it comes to exploiting openings in enemy defenses. By mastering your timing and skill rotations, you can maintain pressure on even the toughest enemies. Here are some additional combat strategies:
