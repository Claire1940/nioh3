## Why Choose a Katana Build in Nioh 3?

In *Nioh 3*, the Katana is one of the most versatile weapons, offering a balanced blend of speed, range, and powerful attacks. Unlike other weapons that require heavy setups or lengthy combo chains, the Katana is quick, precise, and offers immense potential for damage output. The Katana's combination of close-range slashes and fast attack speeds makes it perfect for both single-target and mob control. 

Why does this Katana build matter? Because, with the right stats and skills, you can deal massive amounts of damage while maintaining great mobility. No other weapon allows you to strike as swiftly, deflect with ease, and chain attacks together as effortlessly as the Katana.

## Key Stats to Focus on for Your Katana Build

A *Nioh 3 Katana build* requires careful attention to your stats, ensuring that you maximize your offensive and defensive capabilities. Below are the key stats to focus on:

- **Skill**: This stat boosts the damage output of your Katana, as it directly influences the power of your weapon skills.
- **Heart**: Heart increases your overall Ki and stamina regeneration, making it easier to maintain your attacks and defensive maneuvers.
- **Stamina**: Invest in stamina to avoid running out of Ki during extended fights. This allows you to string together powerful combos without fear of being interrupted.
- **Strength**: For added damage output, Strength works well in tandem with your Katana, increasing your ability to deal heavy damage while wielding the weapon.

It’s essential to prioritize **Skill** first and foremost, as it amplifies your Katana's innate strength. Following that, you can focus on **Heart** and **Stamina** to keep your attacks fluid and consistent.

## Essential Katana Skills to Maximize Your Damage

To truly master a *Nioh 3 katana build*, you need to unlock and perfect key weapon skills. These will increase your attack power and enhance your combat style. Here are some of the best skills to include in your build:

1. **Severing Spin**: A great skill for dealing heavy damage while also hitting multiple enemies at once. It has an impressive AoE (Area of Effect) that is perfect for crowd control.
2. **Fallen Leaf**: This skill increases your ability to chain attacks together, making your combos faster and more damaging.
3. **Shadow Strike**: A powerful move that lets you instantly teleport behind enemies, dealing devastating critical damage.
4. **Whirlwind**: This fast spinning attack can cut down multiple enemies with one smooth motion, making it ideal for sweeping through large mobs.

For your passive skills, focus on boosting your attack power and enhancing your Ki recovery. Abilities like **Ki Pulse** will ensure you can maintain pressure without exhausting yourself.

## Gear and Armor Choices for Maximum Efficiency

When it comes to armor for your *Nioh 3 katana build*, you’ll want to strike a balance between protection and agility. Heavy armor might offer higher defense, but it will slow you down and leave you vulnerable to slower attacks. Aim for **medium armor** that provides a good mix of defense without compromising your speed.

Consider the following gear for the best performance:

- **Kusa-no-Kami Set**: A powerful set for Katana users that increases your damage output while reducing stamina consumption.
- **Tengu’s Master Set**: Increases the speed of your attacks, letting you chain combos faster and deal more damage in the process.
- **Oni-Bane Set**: Boosts your Ki regeneration, which is crucial for maintaining offensive momentum in battle.

### Weapons to Pair with Your Katana

The **dual Katana** is a perfect side weapon to pair with your main Katana, offering high-speed attacks and additional damage to overwhelmed enemies. Alternatively, the **bow** can complement your build by offering ranged attacks to keep enemies at bay before closing in for the kill.

## Combat Tips for Your Nioh 3 Katana Build

Now that you have the ideal stats, skills, and gear, here are some tips for putting your *Nioh 3 Katana build* into action:

1. **Use Ki Pulse to Reset and Attack**: Keep your Ki gauge in check by timing your **Ki Pulse** after every attack. This will recharge your Ki and allow you to keep up your assault without running out of stamina.
2. **Chain Skills for Devastating Combos**: Combine **Severing Spin** with **Fallen Leaf** to keep enemies locked in your combo. The key to a great Katana build is flowing from one skill to the next.
3. **Master Deflecting and Dodging**: The Katana excels at deflecting enemy attacks. Timing your **deflects** can turn the tide of battle, allowing you to counter and deal huge damage when your opponent is vulnerable.
4. **Adapt to Each Situation**: Your Katana build is flexible, so don’t hesitate to switch strategies during battle. If you’re facing a single powerful boss, focus on heavy strikes. For mobs of enemies, use AoE skills like **Whirlwind** to clear them out quickly.
