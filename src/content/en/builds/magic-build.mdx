When building your Onmyo Sorcery character, focusing on the right stats and skills is crucial for maximizing the effectiveness of your magic. Here’s a breakdown of the most important aspects to consider:

- **Skill**: If you’re also incorporating weapon-based abilities into your build, investing in Skill can unlock additional combat techniques that complement your magic abilities.
- **Magic**: Of course, focusing on magic skills will allow you to unlock new Onmyo sorceries. Be sure to invest in the appropriate magic skills to enhance your spellcasting potential.

To maximize your effectiveness with the *Nioh 3 magic build*, it’s important to use a combination of powerful magic spells and solid combat strategies. Here are some top tips to get the most out of your Onmyo Sorcery build:

- **Master the Elements**: Use elemental Onmyo magic like Fire, Water, and Lightning to exploit enemy weaknesses. For example, Lightning works wonders against enemies weak to shock damage, while Fire can burn foes over time.
- **Buff Yourself**: Buffs like *Onmyo Power* or *Light Affinity* can make you stronger and more resilient, helping you withstand incoming damage while increasing your damage output.
- **Use Crowd Control Spells**: Spells like *Divine Confetti* and *Windstorm* can disrupt enemy movements, allowing you to gain control of large groups of enemies or tricky bosses.
- **Combine Magic with Melee**: Though the *Nioh 3 magic build* is focused on spellcasting, blending magic with close-range combat can make you a versatile fighter. Use your magic to soften up enemies before finishing them with melee attacks.
