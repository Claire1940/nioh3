**ALL Nioh 3 Weapons RANKED! Which is Most OP?!**

When it comes to *Nioh 3*, the weapons you wield are crucial to your survival. Choosing the right weapon can make all the difference in a fight, especially when facing the most powerful bosses. But with so many options available, how do you know which one is truly the most overpowered (OP)? In this post, we'll take a deep dive into the *Nioh 3 weapons* tier list, comparing their strengths, weaknesses, and overall impact on gameplay.

### Why Weapon Choice Matters in Nioh 3

In *Nioh 3*, weapons are more than just tools for combat—they are an extension of your playstyle. Whether you're a swift and agile ninja or a hulking samurai, your weapon choice can make or break your strategy. That’s why understanding the best *Nioh 3 weapons* is essential. The right weapon not only improves your damage output but also complements your combat tactics. With this guide, you'll be able to make an informed decision about which weapon suits your playstyle the best.

### 1. Katana: A Samurai's True Power

The **Katana** in *Nioh 3* is a balanced weapon that excels in speed and flexibility. Known for its ability to quickly deal high damage, it's the weapon of choice for many players who favor fast, fluid combat. 

- **Pros**:
  - High DPS (damage per second) due to fast attacks.
  - Strong stance transitions, allowing for versatile combat.
  - Ideal for both offensive and defensive playstyles.

- **Cons**:
  - Requires precise timing to maximize its potential in fights.
  - Not as heavy-hitting as other weapons like the Odachi.

Despite these drawbacks, the **Katana** is widely considered one of the top *Nioh 3 weapons*. It's an excellent choice for those who want a quick and lethal weapon that adapts well to different situations.

### 2. Odachi: The Giant Slayer

The **Odachi** is the weapon for players who enjoy delivering devastating blows and controlling the battlefield. With its long reach and powerful strikes, the **Odachi** can deal with groups of enemies and large bosses alike.

- **Pros**:
  - Massive AoE (Area of Effect) attacks that hit multiple enemies.
  - Strong stagger capabilities, making it effective against large bosses.
  - Perfect for players who prefer heavy, slow-hitting weapons.

- **Cons**:
  - Slow attacks leave you vulnerable if you're not careful.
  - Requires great timing to land hits efficiently.

Due to its raw power, the **Odachi** is a favorite among many veteran *Nioh 3* players. It excels in crowd control and can break through most enemies’ defenses with ease.

### 3. Kusarigama: The Ninja’s Choice

The **Kusarigama** is arguably one of the most unique *Nioh 3 weapons*. Combining the reach of a chain and sickle with the speed of a dagger, it provides players with a highly flexible and effective weapon for crowd control.

- **Pros**:
  - Great for quick, hit-and-run tactics.
  - Allows for fast, uninterrupted attacks.
  - Can pull enemies towards you, making it excellent for setting up combos.

- **Cons**:
  - Can be difficult to master due to its unconventional moveset.
  - Requires good positioning to get the most out of its long-range capabilities.

For **Kusarigama** enthusiasts, this weapon is all about versatility. Its ability to quickly switch between stances makes it a top contender for players who prefer agile, ninja-style combat.

### 4. Spear: The Long Reach

The **Spear** is a versatile weapon known for its ability to control distance. If you prefer staying out of reach while dishing out heavy hits, this is the weapon for you.

- **Pros**:
  - Excellent range and maneuverability.
  - Strong at keeping enemies at bay.
  - Effective against most enemies in both one-on-one and group fights.

- **Cons**:
  - Slower than the Katana, which can leave you open to fast enemies.
  - Requires precision to land critical hits.

Despite being a bit slower, the **Spear** is one of the most reliable weapons in *Nioh 3*. Its ability to keep enemies at a distance and its versatility make it a must-try for players seeking control over the battlefield.

### 5. Dual Swords: Fast and Furious

The **Dual Swords** are a fantastic choice for aggressive players who want to rain down damage quickly and efficiently. Their quick strikes and incredible combo potential make them an ideal choice for fast-paced combat.

- **Pros**:
  - High attack speed with devastating combos.
  - Great for breaking enemy posture.
  - Highly effective for players who like to keep up constant pressure.

- **Cons**:
  - Lacks range, requiring you to get close to enemies.
  - Lower overall damage compared to slower weapons.

The **Dual Swords** are often favored by those who thrive on agility and speed. With the right setup, they can tear through enemies like a whirlwind.

### 6. Greatsword: Heavy Hitter

The **Greatsword** is a slow but extremely powerful weapon. With massive damage potential and wide AoE attacks, it’s the ideal weapon for players who want to dish out incredible damage in fewer, more calculated strikes.

- **Pros**:
  - High damage output with each swing.
  - Wide AoE attacks that hit multiple enemies at once.
  - Can break enemy defenses with ease.

- **Cons**:
  - Slow and vulnerable to fast enemies.
  - Requires good timing and positioning.

For players who enjoy the satisfaction of dealing immense damage with a single blow, the **Greatsword** is a formidable choice in *Nioh 3*.

### 7. Bow: Precision from Afar

The **Bow** is a ranged weapon, making it unique in a game that usually focuses on close combat. Its ability to shoot powerful arrows from a distance can give players a tactical advantage.

- **Pros**:
  - Allows you to attack from a safe distance.
  - Can hit weak spots on enemies, dealing massive damage.
  - Great for picking off enemies before they can get close.

- **Cons**:
  - Requires precision and patience.
  - Less effective in close-quarters combat.

The **Bow** excels in the hands of players who prefer to plan ahead and attack from a distance. If you love precision and want to take down enemies before they reach you, this is the weapon for you.

### Conclusion: Which Weapon Reigns Supreme?

So, which is the most OP *Nioh 3 weapon*? The answer depends on your playstyle. While the **Katana** offers speed and adaptability, the **Odachi** brings sheer power. For those who enjoy agility and precision, the **Kusarigama** or **Dual Swords** could be the perfect choice. If you prefer staying at a distance and picking off enemies, the **Bow** is a great option. Ultimately, the most OP weapon is the one that best fits your combat style.
