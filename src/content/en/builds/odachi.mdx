The Odachi in *Nioh 3* isn’t just another weapon—it’s a colossal blade capable of inflicting devastating damage, if you know how to wield it correctly. ### Why the Nioh 3 Odachi Build is a Game-Changer

When it comes to weapons in *Nioh 3*, the Odachi stands out as one of the most powerful and visually striking choices. This massive sword combines reach, raw power, and devastating combos that make it a force to be reckoned with. But mastering the Odachi isn’t as simple as swinging it around. To truly get the most out of this weapon, you need to optimize your build, from stat distribution to skills and gear. Let’s break down the essential elements of a strong Nioh 3 Odachi build.

### The Best Stats for a Nioh 3 Odachi Build

For an optimal Nioh 3 Odachi build, focusing on the right stats is critical. The primary stats you’ll want to invest in are:

- **Strength**: This is the Odachi’s primary stat, directly influencing the damage output of your attacks. High Strength will allow you to deliver massive hits, especially when using heavy attacks.
- **Skill**: The Skill stat is also important for maximizing your weapon’s effectiveness. It improves your ability to use many of the Odachi’s key skills, such as wide-reaching AoE attacks and unblockable strikes.
- **Stamina**: Stamina is crucial for maintaining mobility with the Odachi. Despite its weight, the weapon allows for fluid movement, especially when you balance your Stamina pool.
- **Dexterity**: Dexterity impacts your ability to perform faster, more precise strikes. It’s useful for improving your skill with the Odachi’s speedier moves.

**Tip**: According to community feedback, putting a heavy emphasis on Strength while maintaining a moderate investment in Skill and Stamina will yield the best results for this build.

### Essential Skills for a Nioh 3 Odachi Build

Now that we’ve covered the essential stats, let's dive into the skills that will help you unlock the full potential of the Nioh 3 Odachi. Here are the key skills you should prioritize:

- **Severing Spin**: One of the best AoE moves in the Odachi's arsenal, this skill allows you to hit multiple enemies in a wide area. It’s perfect for clearing crowds, especially in difficult encounters.
- **The Mighty Strike**: This heavy attack can deal colossal damage when charged up. A must-have for any Odachi user focused on maximizing damage output.
- **Flowing Waters**: This skill enhances your agility while wielding the Odachi. It’s great for improving the flow of your combat, especially when dodging or attacking after a parry.

**Pro Tip**: Players recommend pairing Severing Spin with Mighty Strike for massive damage potential. These two skills can clear waves of enemies and deal devastating damage to bosses.

### Top Armor Sets to Pair with Your Nioh 3 Odachi Build

When it comes to your armor set, it’s essential to strike the right balance between offense and defense. The Nioh 3 Odachi build relies on stamina-heavy attacks and mobility, so you need armor that supports both.

- **Kojirō Armor Set**: This set provides a boost to your Strength and increases your stamina recovery. It's perfect for players who want a good balance of offense and defense.
- **Sakura Armor Set**: Known for its high defense and elemental resistance, this armor is ideal for taking on high-level enemies and bosses.
- **Tengu Set**: If you're looking to maximize your speed and agility, the Tengu set will allow you to move quickly and perform more frequent attacks.

You can mix and match armor sets depending on your playstyle, but these are some of the most recommended choices for an Odachi-focused build.

### Weapons and Accessories to Enhance Your Odachi Build

Your Nioh 3 Odachi build will also benefit from certain accessories and weapon upgrades. Here’s what you should aim for:

- **Odachi Weapons**: Naturally, the best weapon for your build is another Odachi, but you should look for one with a high Strength scaling to maximize damage. Many players opt for the *Fanged Odachi*, which has a built-in bonus for Strength.
- **Amulets and Charms**: Equip charms that enhance your critical hit damage and give you more stamina regeneration. The *Amulet of the Oni* is a fan-favorite for improving your damage output.
- **Accessories**: The *Magatama* accessory is an excellent choice as it boosts your overall weapon effectiveness and provides additional buffs.

**Tip**: Community feedback suggests that players should upgrade their Odachi early and often, focusing on damage-enhancing upgrades like *Divine and Amrita+* for better efficiency in combat.

### Combat Strategy for Your Nioh 3 Odachi Build

The Odachi isn’t just about raw power—it’s about controlling space and managing stamina. Here's a breakdown of the best combat strategies:

1. **Wide-Area Attacks**: Use your Odachi’s reach to clear out groups of enemies. Severing Spin and other AoE skills can deal with multiple enemies at once, while your range keeps you safe from their attacks.
2. **Heavy Attacks**: Charge up your Mighty Strike when facing large bosses or tough enemies. With the right timing, you can stagger and break their defenses easily.
3. **Dodge and Counter**: Don’t forget to dodge and use your counter-attacks. The Odachi’s reach allows you to perform quick, powerful counters that can turn the tide in your favor.

By mastering these strategies and making the right equipment choices, you’ll become a force to be reckoned with in Nioh 3.
