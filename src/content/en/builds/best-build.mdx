### Key Stats for the Best Nioh 3 Build

Understanding which stats to prioritize is fundamental in **Nioh 3**. Depending on your playstyle—whether it's focusing on fast, nimble attacks or heavy, devastating blows—your build will change. Here’s a quick rundown of the most important stats:

- **Heart**: Essential for managing stamina, which affects your ability to perform actions like dodging, attacking, and blocking.
- **Skill**: This stat is critical for weapon proficiency, especially for fast, agile weapons like katanas or dual swords.
- **Body**: A must for tanky builds, this increases your defense and health, allowing you to take more hits.
- **Spirit**: Increases your access to powerful Guardian Spirits, which provide useful buffs and abilities.
  
For a versatile build, consider focusing on **Skill** and **Heart** to balance offense and stamina. If you're aiming for a tankier build, prioritizing **Body** and **Spirit** may be more beneficial.

### Weapons for Your Best Nioh 3 Build

Weapons are the cornerstone of any build in **Nioh 3**. Choosing the right weapon will significantly impact your combat style. Here are some of the most powerful weapon options:

- **Dual Swords**: Known for their speed and aggressive combos, dual swords are perfect for **Nioh 3** players who like to overwhelm enemies quickly. They are an excellent choice for beginners who want to quickly learn the game mechanics.
- **Katana**: A classic choice for both beginners and pros, the katana offers a balanced mix of speed and damage, making it ideal for all types of encounters.
- **Odachi**: If you're looking for power, the Odachi is your go-to weapon. With wide-range, heavy-hitting attacks, it can easily break through enemy defenses, making it great for players with high **Body** stats.
  
Make sure to check out specific builds for **dual swords** if you want a more aggressive setup or **katana** builds if you're aiming for balanced play.

### Best Builds for Beginners

New to **Nioh 3**? Don’t worry, there are several excellent beginner builds that will help you ease into the game. Here are a few tips:

- **Balanced Katana Build**: Focus on **Skill** and **Heart** for a smooth introduction. The katana offers both speed and damage, so you can get the hang of combat mechanics without being overwhelmed.
- **Dual Swords**: If you prefer a more offensive approach, build around **Skill** and **Heart**. The fast pace of dual swords allows you to deal quick, repeated blows, perfect for beginner-friendly action.
- **Tanky Odachi Build**: If you want to withstand more damage, build your character around **Body** and **Spirit**. The Odachi provides slow but powerful attacks, and a tanky build will let you take on enemies head-on without worrying about health.

Whichever option you go for, make sure to focus on upgrading your weapon proficiency and keeping your defense high. This will help you survive even the toughest enemies early on.

### Best Builds for Pros

For more advanced players who want to experiment with high-level builds, consider the following:

- **Max Damage Katana Build**: Aiming for maximum DPS, focus on **Skill** and **Heart** to increase your attack speed and stamina. This build maximizes the katana’s potential, allowing for fast combos and devastating damage output.
- **Speedy Dual Swords**: For an ultra-fast playstyle, focus on **Skill** and **Heart** to make the most of the dual swords’ speed. With this build, you’ll be dodging and striking in quick succession, perfect for taking down multiple enemies at once.
- **Odachi Boss Slayer Build**: For a more powerful approach, the **Body** and **Spirit** stats shine here. With the Odachi, you can dish out massive damage while sustaining yourself through tough battles. Ideal for veteran players tackling endgame content.

Experienced players will find that experimenting with their stats and weapons will yield powerful results, and mastering these builds can significantly improve your performance in **Nioh 3**.
