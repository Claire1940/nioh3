### The Core of the Nioh 3 Ninja Build

To master the Nioh 3 ninja build, you must focus on key elements that enhance stealth and speed. The ninja’s skill set is built to take advantage of quick movements, sneak attacks, and dexterity. The following elements should be prioritized:

- **Stats Allocation**: Focus on Dexterity and Heart for increased agility and stamina.
- **Skills**: Invest in ninja and stealth-related skills to boost your abilities in both offensive and defensive combat.
- **Armor**: Opt for lightweight armor to maintain your speed and mobility without sacrificing too much defense.
- **Weapons**: Dual Katanas or the Kusarigama are perfect for this build, offering fast strikes and devastating combos.

### Optimal Stat Distribution for Stealth and Speed

When creating the best Nioh 3 ninja build, understanding stat distribution is critical. Focus on maximizing Dexterity, as it directly impacts your agility and stealth abilities. Here’s a breakdown of the most important stats for this build:

- **Dexterity**: This should be your highest stat. It enhances your ability to perform fast strikes, improves your stealth, and increases the damage output of ninja tools and weapons.
- **Heart**: Heart increases stamina, allowing you to chain together attacks and abilities without running out of energy.
- **Skill**: Skill boosts your proficiency with weapons like the Katana and Kusarigama, making it essential for maximizing damage output.
- **Stamina**: While not as crucial as Dexterity, Stamina ensures you have enough endurance to keep up the pace during fast-paced combat scenarios.

### Ninja Tools and Skills

Ninja tools are essential in amplifying your stealth capabilities and providing additional offensive power. Some of the most useful ninja tools to include are:

- **Shuriken**: A versatile tool that lets you deal damage from a distance while keeping enemies distracted.
- **Kunai**: Great for staggering opponents and creating openings for critical attacks.
- **Smoke Bombs**: Perfect for escaping sticky situations or evading enemy detection.
- **Oni-bi**: This tool can deal significant damage over time, making it a great option to weaken enemies before engaging them.

As for skills, here are some to prioritize:
- **Increased Stealth**: This ability will allow you to move undetected, making it easier to position yourself for a deadly sneak attack.
- **Double-Edged Katana Skills**: Boosting your katana skills increases your effectiveness in combat, enabling you to dish out rapid, damaging strikes.
- **Kunai and Shuriken Mastery**: Improving these skills ensures that your ranged attacks hit harder and with greater precision.

### Best Armor for the Nioh 3 Ninja Build

For the Nioh 3 ninja build, armor is crucial for maintaining your speed and stealth. Heavy armor will slow you down, so opt for **Light Armor** to keep your mobility high. The key to success is finding armor that doesn’t weigh you down while still providing decent defense. Here are a few armor sets that work well:

- **Tengu Set**: This set is perfect for those focusing on speed and stealth. It boosts your movement speed and critical hit chance, making it a top choice for the ninja build.
- **Obsidian Set**: A great choice for those looking to balance stealth with defense. It boosts your skill proficiency and ninja abilities.
  
Light armor sets like these will give you the agility you need to zip through battlefields and avoid damage while still giving you enough defense to survive tough encounters.

### Combat Strategy: Stealth, Speed, and Execution

Mastering the Nioh 3 ninja build means perfecting your combat strategy. You want to use stealth to get the jump on enemies, then use your speed to stay ahead of them. Here are a few tips to maximize your effectiveness:

- **Use Stealth to Get the Drop on Enemies**: The key to the ninja build is positioning. Take advantage of shadows and high ground to surprise enemies. Use Smoke Bombs to disorient foes and strike when their guard is down.
- **Chain Attacks**: With your focus on speed, you’ll want to chain your attacks together for maximum damage. Use your weapons’ fast attack animations to create relentless combos that overwhelm your enemies.
- **Always Keep Moving**: In battle, staying mobile is essential. Keep your enemies on their toes with quick dashes, double-jumps, and fast rolls.
