The Kusarigama is one of the most versatile and unique weapons in *Nioh 3*, offering players the chance to master both distance and close combat with the power of the chain sickle. ## Why Choose the Kusarigama Build in Nioh 3?

If you’re a fan of agile and fast-paced combat, the Kusarigama build in *Nioh 3* will feel right at home. This weapon allows you to manipulate enemies from a distance, performing both quick strikes and devastating combos. The Kusarigama combines the long reach of the chain with the precision of the sickle, allowing for high mobility, crowd control, and swift evasion. It’s a weapon that rewards skillful use but also offers versatility in combat, making it an excellent choice for both new players and veterans.

Players have reported that mastering the Kusarigama offers an advantage in various scenarios, particularly when dealing with multiple enemies at once. The chain sickle’s range lets you keep enemies at bay while pulling them closer for a finishing blow.

## Best Stats for the Kusarigama Build

When creating your Kusarigama build, focusing on the right stats is crucial for optimizing your performance. Here’s a breakdown of the most important stats to consider:

- **Skill (Primary Stat)**: Kusarigama thrives on high skill. Boosting your Skill stat increases your overall damage output, particularly for the Kusarigama’s unique moves.
- **Heart**: The Heart stat boosts your Ki capacity and your proficiency with certain combat styles. Since the Kusarigama requires quick attacks and mobility, having more Ki will help you chain moves together without running out of energy.
- **Stamina**: Stamina is important for your overall survival and the ability to perform quick dodges. While not as critical as Skill, a well-balanced Stamina stat can help you survive longer in tough battles.
- **Strength**: Strength boosts your defense and increases your ability to wield heavier gear. Though not as important for Kusarigama as Skill or Heart, a moderate amount of Strength can help you equip some useful gear.

## Weapon Skills and Abilities for Kusarigama

The Kusarigama offers some powerful weapon skills that allow you to manipulate both enemies and the battlefield. Mastering these skills will make all the difference in your Kusarigama build. Here are some key skills to focus on:

- **Whirlwind**: This skill is great for clearing out groups of enemies with quick, wide attacks.
- **Chain Grapple**: This move is essential for pulling enemies towards you, making it easy to chain combos or set up for a lethal finisher.
- **Rising Moon**: A strong vertical attack that helps you deal with airborne enemies or those who are dodging your attacks.

In addition to these skills, many *Nioh 3* players recommend focusing on enhancing your Kusarigama’s innate ability to inflict status effects like paralysis or poison, depending on your desired playstyle.

## Gear and Armor for Kusarigama Builds

Choosing the right gear is critical for any *Nioh 3* build, but it’s especially important for a Kusarigama build, where mobility and Ki management are key. Here’s what to look for:

- **Armor**: Light to medium armor is the best choice for Kusarigama builds. Light armor provides the mobility needed to execute rapid attacks and dodges, while medium armor offers a balance of defense and mobility. Avoid heavy armor as it will slow down your movement, which can limit the Kusarigama’s full potential.
- **Weapons**: In addition to your Kusarigama, consider pairing it with a second weapon like a Katana or Spear. Both weapons complement the Kusarigama’s speed and range, allowing you to switch between styles depending on the situation. The *Nioh 3* Odachi build also pairs well, allowing you to deal with tougher enemies when needed.

### Key Gear Stats to Focus On:
- **Ki Damage Reduction**: Helps keep your Ki usage efficient, preventing you from running out of stamina in the middle of a fight.
- **Skill Damage**: Boosts your Kusarigama’s attack power and helps you deal more damage with your abilities.
- **Defensive Affinities**: Look for gear with high elemental resistances or protection against debuffs, as *Nioh 3*’s tougher enemies can apply multiple status effects at once.

## Playing the Kusarigama Build: Tips and Tricks

Now that you’ve equipped the right stats and gear, it’s time to put your Kusarigama build to the test. Here are some tips and tricks to help you dominate the battlefield:

- **Use Chain Grapple Wisely**: The Kusarigama’s chain is a versatile tool for closing gaps and setting up devastating combos. However, be mindful of your surroundings, as enemies can sometimes counter your grapple. Timing is everything.
- **Maximize Speed with Quick Attacks**: The Kusarigama excels at fast, fluid attacks. Use these to overwhelm your enemies and keep them off balance. Avoid slow, heavy attacks, as they can leave you vulnerable.
- **Focus on Ki Management**: The Kusarigama requires precise Ki management. Be sure to balance your attacks with evasive maneuvers to avoid running out of stamina in tough battles. Combine quick dodges with your high mobility to stay in control of the fight.
- **Master the Elemental Affinities**: Many *Nioh 3* bosses and enemies have elemental weaknesses. Equip gear that enhances your Kusarigama’s elemental damage or status effects to exploit these weaknesses for maximum damage output.
