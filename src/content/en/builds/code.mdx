## Unlock Exclusive Rewards in Nioh 3

As one of the most anticipated action RPGs of 2026, **Nioh 3** offers several ways to get exclusive rewards before and after launch. Unlike traditional redeem code systems, Nioh 3 provides **pre-order bonuses**, **early purchase rewards**, and **demo completion items** that can give you a significant advantage in your journey through feudal Japan.

### Pre-Order Bonus: Hellfire Equipment Set

If you **pre-order Nioh 3** before the February 6, 2026 release date, you'll receive the exclusive **Hellfire Equipment Set** along with the **Scampuss Furball Netsuke** charm. This pre-order bonus is available regardless of which edition you purchase.

**What's Included:**
- **Hellfire Equipment Set** - A powerful armor set with unique fire-based properties
- **Scampuss Furball Netsuke** - A special charm accessory that provides bonus effects

The Hellfire Equipment Set is designed to give you an early-game advantage with its balanced stats and fire damage bonuses, making it perfect for players who want to start strong.

### Early Purchase Bonus: Hellfrost Equipment Set

Players who purchase **Nioh 3 by February 19, 2026** (within 13 days of launch) will receive the **Hellfrost Equipment Set** as an early purchase bonus. This is separate from the pre-order bonus, meaning you can get both if you pre-order!

**What's Included:**
- **Hellfrost Warrior Armor Set** - Heavy armor with ice-based defensive properties
- **Hellfrost Ninja Attire Set** - Light armor optimized for stealth and agility

The Hellfrost Equipment Set offers two distinct playstyles, allowing you to choose between the tanky Warrior approach or the swift Ninja style. This versatility makes it an excellent reward for experimenting with different builds early in the game.

### Demo Completion Reward: Mark of the Vanquisher

Following the tradition of previous Nioh games, completing the **Nioh 3 demo** will grant you the **"Mark of the Vanquisher"** DLC item in the full game. This special reward is a badge of honor that shows you've tested your skills before the official release.

**How to Get It:**
1. Download the Nioh 3 demo (available on PS5 and PC)
2. Complete the demo's main objectives
3. The Mark of the Vanquisher will automatically transfer to your full game save

This item typically provides unique cosmetic effects or minor stat bonuses, making it a must-have for collectors and dedicated fans.

**Deluxe Edition**
